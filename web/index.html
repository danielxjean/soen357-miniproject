The Problem

In today’s society, digital devices are at arms reach. Whether we like it or not, electronic devices have become a major part of our lives. Whether it be to order food, chat with a friend or watch a movie, the average amount of screen time is 11 hours per day in North America. The number is only getting worse with the onset of the Covid-19 pandemic which forced many of us to move our daily activities to the digital screens. In fact, it is estimated that around 42% of people have shifted to working from home and many students are also being taught through a screen. While these devices can help us feel less alone by communicating with friends and family or keep us entertained, it is important to understand how these devices can interfere with our physical and mental health.

The Color Palette

We thought of the color of green which symbolizes nature and the natural world. It is also known for being associated to many other subjects such as good luck, health or even money. The idea of health and nature lead us to choose this color palette because of its calming effect and its association to nature. We thought that this can help connect the user to the outside world around them during this pandemic.

The Typography

We chose the Open Sans designed by Steve Matteson. It is the second most popular typeface on Google's registry of fonts. This means that is is highly recognizable and therefore, familiar to the users.
